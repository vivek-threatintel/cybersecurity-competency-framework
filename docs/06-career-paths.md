# Career Strategy Principles

- Depth > Breadth  
- Lab Proof > Certificate Badge  
- Research > Passive Consumption  
- Public Portfolio > Resume Claims  
- Specialization after Foundation  

Avoid becoming a “tool operator.”  
Aim to become a systems thinker.
